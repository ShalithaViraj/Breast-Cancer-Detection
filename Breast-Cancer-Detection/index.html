Breast Cancer

Get To Know About Breast Cancer

Breast cancer is a frequently occurring malignancy that originates in the breast's tissues. It can affect both women and, though less commonly, men. This cancer often begins in the milk ducts or lobules and may manifest as a lump or other changes in the breast. Early detection through self-exams and medical screenings is pivotal for timely and effective treatment, contributing to improved survival rates.

Hybrid Access Control

Get To Know About Access Control

The RBAC-ABAC-MFA framework redefines access control with enhanced granularity and adaptability, outperforming traditional Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). It incorporates user attributes like role, department, location, and time for heightened security and flexibility. Furthermore, the system bolsters security through multi-factor authentication (MFA), employing diverse authentication methods to deter unauthorized access. Moreover, it extends these advantages to file sharing authorization, using user attributes to restrict access to specific files and resources, thus fortifying data security and reducing the risk of breaches. This holistic approach strikes a harmonious balance between security and efficiency, making it particularly valuable in healthcare settings and beyond by facilitating swift, controlled access to sensitive information while preserving patient privacy.

Blockchain

Get To Know About Blockchain

Traditional healthcare systems have difficulties such as limited patient access to medical records, poor communication, and low patient participation, which may negatively impact patients' health. The decentralized digital ledger blockchain can solve these problems by providing secure and transparent transactions, efficient healthcare provider-patient communication, and safeguards medical records. Automating appointment scheduling, prescription reminders, and test results with smart contracts improves patients' user experience and medical information control. Through utilizing the system, healthcare practitioners can provide more efficient and effective care by revolutionizing healthcare operations by increasing the satisfaction of patients, access to superior care, and reducing costs.

The Hybrid Intrusion Prevention System (HIPS) represents an innovative and comprehensive cybersecurity solution poised to fortify networked environments against a diverse and evolving array of cyber threats. At its core, the HIPS combines the prowess of an advanced anomaly-based Intrusion Detection System (IDS) with a highly intuitive Graphical User Interface (GUI) tailored for the configuration and management of Snort, a renowned open-source intrusion detection and prevention tool. And, two types of IDS based on the approach are rule-based and anomaly-based. The rule-based approach is act as a filter that work for a given rules and etc. In contrast, the anomaly-based approach establishes a baseline of normal patterns and can detect previously unseen attacks.
