Research Problem

Strong Access Control SECURITY

Strong access control is crucial for healthcare systems to safeguard patient privacy, prevent data breaches, and ensure compliance with regulations. It restricts unauthorized access, protects sensitive medical information, and maintains trust in the confidentiality and integrity of healthcare data.

Blockchain-Based Data Security

Patient privacy and confidentiality are the key pillars of healthcare data security. Over the years several data breaches occurred due to the centralized storage. Hence, Implementing secure storage for storing Electronic Health Records (EHR) using blockchain technology, will ensure the required confidentiality, integrity, and availability of patient data.

Image processing for Breast Cancer

A image processing CAD accurate system for breast cancer diagnosis. The system developed to automatically identifies tumors in mammography images using CNNs and SVMs. Our user-friendly web application enables accessible early detection of cancer, improving survival rates and quality of life.

Hybrid Security solution for the network

In existing healthcare systems, there is no any network monitoring and mitigating solution. SO I'm going to design and validate a hybrid intrusion prevention system that can adapt to evolving attack techniques and effectively prevent and monitor all types of attacks on the organization's infrastructure

Proposed Solution

Our system has been meticulously designed to cater to the needs of breast cancer patients, providing them with a robust tool for discerning between benign and malignant breast cancer conditions. The primary objective is to create an accessible online platform, facilitating individuals who may be unable to physically consult with medical professionals at a hospital to present their mammography images. Moreover, this platform offers reassurance to patients who harbor doubts regarding their doctors' diagnoses, as they can now independently validate the results through our website. To further enhance the quality of service, we have established a dedicated portal through which users can connect with our specialized medical practitioners.

The development of a system addressing such a sensitive issue as breast cancer necessitates the implementation of a rigorous authentication system to ensure the utmost security and confidentiality of patients' personal information. Establishing a foundation of trust is paramount to encourage patients to willingly share their private details, knowing that their data is safeguarded.

In addition to stringent security measures, our system incorporates a private blockchain architecture to fortify the protection of user and system data, fortifying it against potential data breaches. The presence of blockchain technology in our database not only bolsters the system's security but also fosters user confidence, assuring them that their private data remains invulnerable to external threats.

To shield against potential cyber threats and attacks, our system also features a comprehensive network trafficking system, poised to detect and thwart any unauthorized access attempts or malicious intrusions. This layered approach to security ensures that users can access the website with peace of mind, confident that their private information remains protected from external risks.

Image Processing for Breast Cancer

This research aims to advance breast cancer detection through image processing and machine learning. Objectives include enhancing the CNN model's performance and generalizability, securing patient information, improving prediction explainability, verifying clinical applicability, enabling multi-modality data integration, tracking tissue changes over time, optimizing cost-effective deployment, and enhancing patient education and engagement. These goals collectively drive improved breast cancer diagnosis, speed, and patient outcomes.

Light-Weight Blockchain Storage

This study intends to provide a data storage and management strategy that meets standards and improves healthcare security by Utilizing a lightweight blockchain foundation and smart contracts. This technology is projected to improve healthcare delivery by providing secure and efficient patient data security, data access, reducing wait times, and increasing operational efficiency. The system will comply with HIPAA and GDPR to protect patient privacy and data to adhere to the compliance regulations. The proposed technology is scalable, allowing remote healthcare services to be expanded. Hence the suggested approach seeks to alleviate centralized healthcare system challenges.

Hybrid Access Control

The RBAC-ABAC-MFA framework redefines access control with enhanced granularity and adaptability, outperforming traditional Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). It incorporates user attributes like role, department, location, and time for heightened security and flexibility. Furthermore, the system bolsters security through multi-factor authentication (MFA), employing diverse authentication methods to deter unauthorized access. Moreover, it extends these advantages to file sharing authorization, using user attributes to restrict access to specific files and resources, thus fortifying data security and reducing the risk of breaches. This holistic approach strikes a harmonious balance between security and efficiency, making it particularly valuable in healthcare settings and beyond by facilitating swift, controlled access to sensitive information while preserving patient privacy.

Hybrid intrusion prevention and detection system

My main objective is to develop and evaluate a comprehensive security solution that can effectively prevent, detect, and monitor all types of attacks on the organization's infrastructure. The hybrid intrusion prevention system should be designed to adapt to evolving attack techniques and minimize the occurrence of false positives and false negatives. Additionally, the system should be capable of integrating monitoring tools to provide comprehensive infrastructure monitoring. Ultimately, the main objective of your research is to contribute to the development of a more effective and efficient security solution for organizations to protect their critical assets in a rapidly evolving threat landscape.
